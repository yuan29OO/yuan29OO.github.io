Project 1: Character Design

Design and create a full-body character art with Adobe Illustrator.

In the first part of the project, I brainstormed own original characters and thought about personality for everyone.
In the second part, I picked one character Yuki that I really liked and began to explore different poses for him.
In the first two parts, I did multiple sketches on the design process and exploration of different poses.
Once I came up with a pose that best-describes my character, I created a character art in full body digitally with Adobe Illustrator.
